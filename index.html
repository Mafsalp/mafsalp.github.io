About

A Note On How I
Approach the Work

Most companies treat IT infrastructure as overhead. The strongest organisations recognise it as load-bearing.

Whatever you build today, the business will live with for the next ten years. Build it carefully. Trust before scale, always.

Over the past decade in the UAE, I have built cybersecurity programmes from scratch, migrated thousands of users to Microsoft 365, designed greenfield Azure environments, and rolled out Zero Trust architectures. Today I lead infrastructure and cloud for a multi-entity holding group, where the design decisions are mine to make from day one rather than inherit.

My role sits between the people building the systems and the people funding them. The work, in either direction, is keeping technical reality and commercial intent properly connected.
